HOW TO PLAY

COSTS & DEPLOYMENTS

• Start Setup phase with $800 to buy and deploy units.

• Unit costs: Pawn $100, Knight/Bishop $300, Rook $500, Queen $900.

• Each turn, you can EITHER Move/Attack OR Deploy a shop unit.

• Deployed pieces cannot be moved on the same turn they are placed.

FOG OF WAR & VISION

• The board is covered in fog. You can only see squares within the vision range of your own pieces.

• Vision Ranges:

- Rook: 3 squares (excellent scout!)

- Queen, Knight, Bishop, Pawn, King: 1 square

• Hidden enemy pieces in the fog are completely invisible.

AMBUSHES & KINGS

• AMBUSH: If a sliding unit (Rook, Bishop, Queen) moves through a hidden enemy square, it is blocked, stopped, and loses its turn!

• KING SPAWN: The King starts off-board and automatically spawns randomly in your deploy zone on Turn 5.

• FOG CHECK: Check only triggers if the attacker is visible in your fog. You can escape check using visible information.
